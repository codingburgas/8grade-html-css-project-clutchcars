What is the minimum age to rent?

The minimum age to rent a vehicle from Clutch Cars is 21 years old. Additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.

Can I drop off the car at a different location?

Yes, one-way rentals are available at Clutch Cars. Additional fees may apply depending on the locations.

Is insurance included in the rental?

Basic insurance coverage is included. You may also choose additional coverage options for extra protection.

What is the fuel policy?

Cars are provided with a full tank of fuel. Please return the car with a full tank to avoid refueling charges.

Do you offer roadside assistance?

Yes, Clutch Cars provides 24/7 roadside assistance for all rentals to ensure you're covered in case of emergencies.

Can I rent a car without a credit card?

Yes, Clutch Cars accepts debit cards as well, but additional identification and a security deposit may be required.

Are there mileage limits?

Most rentals from Clutch Cars come with unlimited mileage. However, some special vehicles may have mileage restrictions.

Can someone else drive the rental car?

No, only the individual listed on the rental agreement is authorized to drive the vehicle. Allowing an unauthorized driver is a violation of the rental terms.

How do I extend my rental period?

You can extend your rental period by contacting Clutch Cars before your scheduled return time. Additional charges will apply.
